Classic Cheeseburger

A classic, simple cheeseburger

A delicious cheeseburger on a brioche bun!

Ingredients:

  • 8 brioche buns
  • 2 1/4 lb of ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup of pickles
  • 1/2 cup of lettuce
  • 1 large tomato sliced
  • 1 slice cheddar cheese
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup

Steps:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine ground beef and salt and mix until combined
  2. Lightly wet hands to hsape the mixture into eight patties
  3. Add oil to a non-stick pan and cook burgers for 3-4 minutes per side
  4. Assemble with the brioche buns, tomato slice, lettuce, cheese,pickles, ketchup, and mustard
