Coconut Shrimp

Crispy coconut shrimp ready in minutes!


Crispy, delicious coconut shrimp made with simple ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 lb of shrimp - extra large with shells
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded coconut flakes
  • 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup oil

For the Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons siracha
  • 2 tablespoons honey

Steps:

  1. In one bowl, add flour, salt, and pepper. In a separate bowl, add the two eggs and in the third bowl, add the shredded coconut and bread crumbs
  2. Dip the shrimp in the flour, everything except the tail. Shake off the excess, then dip in the egg mix, then the coconut mix. Repeat the processs until all the shrimp is battered
    3. Add the oil to a deep pot, once heated, add the shrimp in batches for about 2-3 minutes until golden
  3. Serve with coconut shrimp sauce. To make the sauce, whisk the ketchup, siracha and honey together
