Stuffed Bell Pepper Recipe

Delicious stuffed bell peppers with melted cheese and bacon

Stuffed bell peppers is a simple and quick recipe that anyone can enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 3 Stuffed bell peppers
  • 1lb of ground chicken
  • 1 jar of sundried tomatoes
  • 1 jar of pimento olives, sliced
    • 1 to 2 bags of shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 pack of bacon bits. This is optional.
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Your choice of sauce

Steps:

  1. Cut the stuffed bell pepper in half and place in a baking dish
  2. On medium heat, add one table spoon of olive oil
  3. Once the pot is hot enough, add the ground chicken
  4. Once the chicken is fully cooked, add the pimento olives and sun dried tomatoes. Let cook for 2 minutes
  5. Set stove to low heat and let the mixture simmer for 5 to 10 minute.
  6. Set oven to 350-375 degrees Farenheit
  7. Take the ground chicken mixture and add it to each bell pepper
  8. Generously top the bell peppers with the sharp cheddar cheese
  9. Optionally, put the final topping of bacon bits on top of the cheese
  10. Place the bell peppers in the oven for 5-8 minutes
  11. Add your favorite sauce and enjoy!
